Here are the answers:

1. **Three movies make up the Back to the Future franchise**:
	* Back to the Future (1985)
	* Back to the Future Part II (1989)
	* Back to the Future Part III (1990)
2. The main character who invents the time machine is **Marty McFly**, played by Michael J. Fox.
3. The time-traveling vehicle was a **DeLorean DMC-12**.
4. The DeLorean runs on **88 octane gasoline**, which is necessary to reach the speed of 88 miles per hour required for time travel.
5. In the first film, Marty and his friend Doc Brown go back in time to **1955**.
