1. The Back to the Future franchise consists of three movies: Back to the Future (1985), Back to the Future Part II (1989), and Back to the Future Part III (1990).

2. The main character who invents the time machine is named Dr. Emmett Brown, portrayed by Christopher Lloyd.

3. The type of vehicle used as a time machine is a modified DeLorean DMC-12.

4. The time machine in Back to the Future runs on 1.21 gigawatts of power. This power was initially provided by a lightning strike, but later sources of power include Mr. Fusion (a portable nuclear reactor disguised as a garbage can).

5. In the first film, they go to the year 1955.
