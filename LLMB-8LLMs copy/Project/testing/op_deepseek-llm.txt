The Back to the Future franchise consists of three movies: Back to the Future (1985), Back to the Future Part II (1989), and Back to the Future Part III (1990). The main character who invents the time machine is a scientist named Dr. Emmett "Doc" Brown, played by Christopher Lloyd. The time machine is a DeLorean sports car modified with electric parts for its time travel capabilities. It runs on a combination of 1.21 joules of lightning energy and advanced technology. In the first film, they go to the year 1955.
